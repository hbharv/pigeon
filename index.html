Pigeon

I'm not beautiful like a dove. Neither am I graceful nor peaceful like one. I adapt to changing environments, like a pigeon.

I've had the great fortune to travel extensively for a woman of Appalachain means. I'm not athletically gifted, or particularly smart. I just enjoy trying new experiences. I created this website as a means to share those experiences with others, to inspire, to inform, or just to distract. Just don't try to pigeon-hole me.
