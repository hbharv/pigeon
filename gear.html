What gear does a Pigeon need?

The most important lesson I've learned when adventure traveling, is that there is rarely something you need that you can't find where you are. Exceptions include tubeless bicycle tires; however, I don't recommend using them for that reason. An accommodating mindset provides more than the well-provisioned traveler. As a child participant in Oddysey of the Mind challenges, our unofficial motto was "Duct tape holds the world together!" Even a sizable hole can't contend with the world.
